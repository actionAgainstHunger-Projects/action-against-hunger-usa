Our Work in the Field

Combating Child Malnutrition

We work tirelessly to identify and treat severe acute malnutrition in young children, providing therapeutic food and care to save lives.

Our programs also focus on long-term prevention through education, health support, and access to clean water and sanitation.

Empowering Communities Through Agriculture

We empower farmers with the tools and training they need to grow their own food, build sustainable livelihoods, and become more resilient to climate shocks.

This includes providing drought-resistant seeds, teaching new farming techniques, and helping communities manage their water resources effectively.

Providing Clean Water and Sanitation

Access to safe drinking water and proper sanitation is critical to preventing disease and malnutrition. We work in camps and communities to ensure people have what they need to stay healthy.

Our teams install water systems, provide hygiene kits, and run health and sanitation awareness campaigns to combat the spread of illness.
